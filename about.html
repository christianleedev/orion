About Us

Orion Electronic is located in Langkawi, we provide a wide range of electrical appliances and services to meet all of your electrical needs. Our shop offers a diverse selection of high-quality electrical appliances such as air conditioners, refrigerators, televisions, and much more. We pride ourselves on offering a wide selection of products from leading brands at competitive prices.

Repair and installation services

Our commitment to quality extends beyond our products to our services. We offer a variety of services including repair services for appliances such as TV, refrigerators, air conditioners, washing machines and many more. Our experienced technicians are always ready to diagnose and fix any problems with your appliances quickly and efficiently.

In addition to our repair services, we also provide installation services for CCTV cameras, alarm systems, auto gates, barrier and sound systems. Our installation team has extensive experience and training to ensure that your new equipment is installed correctly and functions flawlessly. Check available services here.

History

Orion Electronic was established in 1993 as a repair shop for electrical products in Kedawang, Langkawi. As time passed, we diversified our business by offering electrical product sales and installation services such as CCTV and alarm systems. Today, after 30 years of dedicated service, we have reached a significant milestone. We are immensely grateful to our valuable customers who have played a crucial role in our success. Our commitment to providing the best possible service remains unwavering, and we continue to strive towards improving ourselves every day.
